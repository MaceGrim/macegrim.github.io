About Me

I'm a Sicangu Lakota data scientist and educator passionate about using technology to serve Indigenous communities. Based in Rapid City, South Dakota, I work at the intersection of AI, education, and cultural preservation.

My work focuses on developing AI solutions for tribal communities, teaching data science to Indigenous students, and ensuring that technological advancement includes Indigenous perspectives and values.
