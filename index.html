About

AI that serves people, not the other way around.

I'm Mason Grimshaw -- husband, dad to 4, Sicangu Lakota from the Rosebud Reservation, and a Data Scientist/AI Engineer at Ode Partners.

I'm the Vice President of IndigiGenius, where we empower Indigenous communities in computer science and machine learning, and a co-founder of the Lakota AI Code Camp.

I work at the intersection of enterprise AI systems and community-centered technology. Every project starts with the same question: what problem are we actually solving?

Enterprise AI

Production-grade AI systems that deliver measurable value. Strategy, architecture, and implementation.

Tribal AI

Community-led technology for Indigenous nations. Sovereignty-first, capacity building, practical tools.

Deep Dives

Technical explorations and experiments. Building things from scratch to understand how they work.
